Garage Door Cable & Roller Repair

Replace frayed cables and worn rollers to ensure smooth, quiet door movement.

Schedule this service

Overview

Cables and rollers endure constant wear. Frayed cables can snap, causing door imbalance and safety hazards. We replace cables with aircraft-grade material and install nylon or steel rollers for quieter, smoother operation.

What's included

  • Eliminates grinding and squeaking noises
  • Prevents cable snapping and door dropping
  • Extends life of springs and tracks
  • Improves overall door alignment

How it works

1

Inspection

Check cables for fraying, rollers for wear or damage, and track condition.

2

Replacement

Remove old cables/rollers, install new parts, and adjust tension.

3

Lubrication

Apply professional-grade lubricant to rollers, hinges, and tracks for smooth operation.

Common questions

How often should rollers be replaced?

Every 5–7 years for residential doors, or sooner if noisy or sticky.

Can a snapped cable be repaired?

Yes, we replace the cable and inspect the entire system for safety.
