Garage Door Spring Repair

Safe and precise torsion and extension spring replacement to restore balance and function.

Schedule this service

Overview

Broken springs are one of the most common garage door issues. Our technicians safely replace torsion and extension springs with high-cycle, durable parts. We follow strict safety protocols to prevent injury and ensure smooth operation.

What's included

  • Restores door balance and reduces strain on opener
  • High-cycle springs last longer (10,000+ cycles)
  • Prevents further damage to cables and tracks
  • Same-day service available

How it works

1

Inspection

We assess spring type, tension, and condition. Identify any related issues.

2

Replacement

Use winding bars and proper techniques to safely install new springs.

3

Testing

Test door balance, auto-reverse, and overall operation. Adjust as needed.
