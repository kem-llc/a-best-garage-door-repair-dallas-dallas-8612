Garage Door Cable Repair

Fix frayed or broken cables to ensure safe door movement.

Schedule this service

Overview

Cables are critical for lifting your garage door. If they snap or fray, the door can become dangerous. We replace cables and inspect the entire system for wear.

What's included

  • Restores safe operation
  • Prevents door from jamming
  • Extends door life
  • Includes safety inspection

How it works

1

Inspection

We check cables, drums, and tracks for damage.

2

Replacement

We install new high-strength cables and adjust tension.

3

Testing

We run the door to ensure smooth and balanced movement.

Common questions

How do I know if my cables are bad?

Look for fraying, uneven door movement, or the door not opening fully.

Can I drive with a broken cable?

No, a broken cable can cause the door to fall. Call for repair immediately.
