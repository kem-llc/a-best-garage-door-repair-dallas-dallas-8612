Overview

Whether your opener is noisy, unresponsive, or completely dead, we can help. We repair all major brands—Chamberlain, LiftMaster, Genie—and install new models with Wi-Fi, battery backup, and quiet operation.

What's included

  • Silent operation with belt-drive options
  • Smartphone control and remote access
  • Battery backup for power outages
  • Safety sensors and auto-reverse tested

How it works

1

Diagnosis

Test power, sensors, remote, and internal components to pinpoint the fault.

2

Repair or Replace

Repair if cost-effective; otherwise recommend and install a suitable replacement.

Setup & Test

Program remotes, keypad, and smart features. Verify safety functions.
