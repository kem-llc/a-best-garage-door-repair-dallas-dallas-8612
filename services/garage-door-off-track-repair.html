Garage Door Off-Track Repair

Realign your door back on track quickly and safely.

Schedule this service

Overview

An off-track door is a safety hazard and can damage your property. Our technicians carefully realign the door, repair any bent tracks, and ensure smooth operation.

What's included

  • Prevents further damage
  • Restores functionality
  • Improves safety
  • Extends door lifespan

How it works

1

Assessment

We inspect tracks, rollers, and hinges for damage.

2

Realignment

We carefully lift the door back onto the track and adjust.

3

Final Check

We test the door multiple times to ensure proper alignment.

Common questions

What causes a door to come off track?

Common causes include broken cables, bent tracks, or hitting the door with a vehicle.

Is off-track repair covered by insurance?

Often yes, if caused by an accident. Check with your provider.
